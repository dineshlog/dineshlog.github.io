ISO 27001:2022 — At a Glance
The international standard for Information Security Management Systems (ISMS)
ISO/IEC 27001:2022 specifies the requirements for establishing, implementing, maintaining and continually improving an ISMS within the context of the organisation. Annex A references 93 information security controls derived from ISO/IEC 27002:2022, organised into 4 themes.

ISO 27001:2022 — Clauses 4 to 10
Mandatory requirements that an organisation must meet to achieve certification
Clauses 1–3 are introductory (Scope, Normative references, Terms & definitions). Clauses 4–10 are mandatory certification requirements mapped to the PDCA cycle.

ISO 27002:2022 — Implementation Guidance
Guidance on implementing, maintaining and improving controls
ISO/IEC 27002:2022 provides a reference set of information security controls including implementation guidance. It uses the same 93 controls grouped by 4 themes, with defined attributes for filtering and categorisation.

The 4 Control Themes
ISO 27001:2022 organises all 93 controls into 4 themes replacing the 14 domains of the 2013 edition
